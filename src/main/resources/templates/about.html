About BhootDaya

BhootDaya is a community-driven animal welfare platform built on compassion and technology. We connect people who find stray animals with those who want to give them a forever home.

Our Project SOSO (Save Our Stray Others) initiative makes it simple to report stray animals and browse animals that need adoption — all in one place.

Every animal deserves love. Every adoption changes two lives — the animal's and yours.
